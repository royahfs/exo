About EXO

Exo is a South Korean-Chinese boy group based in Seoul. This group consists of nine members: Suho, Baekhyun, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, Sehun, Xiumin, Lay, and Chen. This group was formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012
